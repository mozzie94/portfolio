Crab War

2024-2025

Crab War is an idle RPG where you control an army of mighty crabs. Battle against ruthless Reptiles, gain resources, upgrade your crabs and evolve them into more vicious species.

Reclaim the lost crab homeland and drive the enemies back to where they came from.
