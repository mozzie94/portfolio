Claw Stars

Claw Stars is a casual game where players grab items with a claw, just like a claw machine. Grab valuable resources and rescue animals from each planet to bring back to life again.

Platform: Android, iOS

Genre: Casual

Released: 2021

Crab War

Crab War is an idle RPG where you control an army of mighty crabs. Battle against ruthless Reptiles, gain resources, upgrade your crabs and evolve them into more vicious species. Reclaim the lost crab homeland and drive the enemies back to where they came from.

Platform: Android, iOS

Genre: Idle

Released: 2016

Light a Way

Light a Way is a casual, idle RPG where you take on the role of the Guardian of Light. Equipped with a staff known as the Weapon of Light, gather loyal allies and powerful upgrades to banish the Darkness and restore the Light.

Platform: Android, iOS

Genre: Idle

Released: 2018
