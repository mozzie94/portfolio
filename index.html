About Me

My name is James Lee, a Game Designer with 9 years under my belt. My humble beginning starts from studying for a Bachelors Degree in Game Development and progressively learning from joining game jams and working.

Although I’ve normally worked as a generalist, I personally felt more attuned to Gameplay, Systems and UX design.

Claw Stars

Claw Stars is a casual game where players grab items with a claw, just like a claw machine. Grab valuable resources and rescue animals from each planet to bring back to life again.

Platform: Android, iOS

Genre: Casual

Engine: Unity

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • System Design
    • Balanced the core gameplay loop progression.
    • Balanced the goals and rewards for events.
  • Gameplay Design
    • Conceptualised and designed multiple LiveOps events.
    • Conceptualised and designed a guild system to improve the game’s community aspect.
    • Improved multiple systems including the friend function, player profile and other base game systems.
  • UX Design
    • Created mock-ups and wireframes for multiple gameplay systems and LiveOp events.
